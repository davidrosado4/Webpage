AI & COMPLEX DYN.

AI meet Complex Dynamics

I have undertaken a personal and original project that endeavors to merge my two beloved domains in science: Artificial Intelligence and Complex Dynamics. I am utilizing AI and the Newton's method to discover all the roots of a polynomial with minimal initial conditions. Although certain theoretical advancements (check here) have been achieved, practical applications have not yet been realized. The idea is to use a ML model to find a probability distribution on the roots of a polynomial and then be used as an initial guess for the Newton method. This means that, with the aid of AI, we could find a neighbourhood of probability in which a polynomial root can be discovered, allowing for a certain margin of error. Subsequently, the Newton method can be applied to find the precise root. This concept promises to bestow a multitude of numerical benefits upon root finding algorithms!

In the near future, I will be launching a GitHub repository dedicated to this project.
