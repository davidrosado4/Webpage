Student Researcher

During my first year in the dual Masters program for Advanced Mathematics and Data Science, I had the unique opportunity to engage in concurrent employment as a student researcher. The University of Barcelona bestowed upon me the coveted MASTER+UB grant, which not only provided financial support but also served as a catalyst for pursuing a Master's degree while fostering a scientific vocation within a research group.

Through this remarkable grant, I was granted entry into the esteemed Holomorphic Dynamics research group, where I had the privilege of collaborating with Xavier Jarque i Ribera. This remarkable opportunity paved the way for the culmination of my master's journey in mathematics, as I embarked on my final thesis under Xavier Jarque i Ribera's guidance.

The final thesis will be available here in the near future.
